Melbourne Public Library has been in operation since 1892, serving the community of Melbourne and its immediate neighbouring suburbs with a quality selection of hard to find and popular titles available for reference.


A Floorplan of the Library is viewable here so you can navigate the area with ease when first visiting.


You can hover over each area to obtain descriptions, or alternatively you can click on an item to have the information displayed below the floor plan.


The clickable items are included as a working feature for all resolutions, including mobile and tablet devices.
